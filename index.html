What is Touch-any?

Touch-any is an Arduino-based product that simply uses PIR sensors to detect hand's position and motion to bring touch and gesture control to any HDTV or projector with a cost of a video game. Whenever you are controlling your home theater or performing a presentation in front of a projector screen, using your hand to navigate through TV channels, choose movies, or even draw on screen are all awesome ways to amaze your friends, maximize presentation experience. This product supports multiple-touch and gestures. One more amazing thing, you will not actually touch your TV or projector screen that keeps your screen clean.

How Touch-any works?

By mounting a frame of sensor strip around your screen, your finger position related to screen corner can be detected. The precision of your finger's position depends on the density of the sensor strip and the screen's size. Touch-any comes with different package that you can choose the most suitable for your TV.

Touch-any communicate with your TV by two manners:

  • It can replicate your TV's remote control. For instance, wiping gesture can be configured to change TV channel or volume.
  • Touch-any also have a USB interface to connect to your computer which can act as a computer mouse or touch pad.

FAQ

What's PIR?
PIR stands for passive infrared sensor which is an electronic sensor that measures infrared (IR) light radiating from objects in its field of view (Wikipedia)
Is there any limit on screen size?
Yes, actually. Touch-any works best on screen from 24" to 80". Bigger screen requires more sensors while smaller screen is too density to control by your hand.
Do I need a smart-TV or an HTPC in order to use Touch-any?
No, you don't. Touch-any has an interface in order to output IR signal that replicates TV's remote control. However, you will be limited on the functions provided by your TV. If you have an HTPC, Touch-any can replace your mouse or touch pad..
